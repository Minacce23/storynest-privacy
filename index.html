Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At story nest, we believe in building trust with parents who use our app to create magical voice experiences for their children. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your family's privacy. We've written this in plain language so you can understand exactly how your data is handled.

What Data We Collect

story nest collects minimal information needed to create personalized narrations:

  • Voice Recordings: When you record your voice for voice cloning, we securely process these recordings through ElevenLabs (our voice processing partner) to create a voice model that sounds like you.
  • App Usage Preferences: We store your app settings locally on your device, such as preferred story categories, playback settings, and other choices you make while using story nest.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, or any other personal information. We do not create accounts or require you to sign in.

How We Use Voice Data

Your voice recordings are sent only to ElevenLabs for voice cloning purposes. We do not store voice samples on our servers or use them for any other purpose. Once ElevenLabs creates your voice clone model, the original recording is deleted.

Here's how the process works:

  • You record your voice within the story nest app
  • The recording is securely sent to ElevenLabs' servers for voice model training
  • ElevenLabs uses AI to create a digital clone of your voice
  • Your voice clone is used to narrate children's stories in your voice
  • The final narrated story is saved only on your device
  • Original voice samples are not retained by story nest

ElevenLabs as Our Voice Processing Partner

story nest uses ElevenLabs' API to process voice recordings and generate voice-cloned narrations. ElevenLabs is a trusted third-party service that handles voice data according to their own privacy practices.

For details about how ElevenLabs uses and protects voice data, please review their privacy policy at https://elevenlabs.io/privacy.

We recommend reviewing ElevenLabs' privacy terms before recording your voice. If you have concerns about their practices, you can contact us before proceeding.

Local Storage on Your Device

All processed content stays on your iPhone. Specifically:

  • Narrated Stories: Generated stories with your voice are stored only on your device
  • Preferences: App settings and preferences are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not back up your stories or preferences to cloud servers
  • Full Control: You can delete any story or clear all data at any time directly from the app

We Don't Sell Your Data

We want to be crystal clear: we do not sell, trade, rent, or share your voice recordings, app preferences, or any other personal information with third parties for marketing or any other purpose. Your data belongs to you.

The only exception is ElevenLabs, which we use to process voice data as described above. We do not share data with any other third parties.

Children's Privacy & COPPA Compliance

Important note: story nest is designed for use by parents, not children. Parents record their own voices and control the app. While the generated stories are created for children to enjoy, the app does not collect any information from children directly.

We are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) because:

  • Parents operate the app and record their own voices
  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • We do not track or identify children using the app
  • Children are listeners, not data subjects in our system

If you have any concerns about children's privacy while using story nest, please contact us.

In-App Purchases & Payment Data

story nest uses Apple's in-app purchase system for subscriptions. When you make a purchase:

  • Apple Handles Payments: Your credit card, Apple ID, and billing information are processed directly by Apple, not by us
  • We Don't See Payment Details: story nest never receives or stores your payment information
  • Apple's Privacy: For details about how Apple handles your payment data, review Apple's Privacy Policy

We only receive information from Apple about whether your subscription is active, so we can enable premium features in the app.

Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data in story nest:

  • You can delete any narrated story at any time
  • You can clear all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Your voice model with ElevenLabs can be deleted by contacting ElevenLabs directly
  • Voice preferences stored locally are removed when you clear the app data

If you'd like assistance with data deletion or have questions, please contact us at the address below.

Security & Data Protection

We take the security of your voice recordings seriously. Here's how we protect your data:

  • Voice recordings are encrypted during transmission to ElevenLabs
  • We do not store voice recordings on our servers
  • App preferences are stored securely on your device using iOS standard encryption
  • We regularly review our security practices to keep your information safe

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we work hard to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links

This privacy policy includes links to ElevenLabs' and Apple's privacy policies. We are not responsible for the practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date below. Your continued use of story nest after changes means you accept the updated policy.
