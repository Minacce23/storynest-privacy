Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 23, 2026

storynest ("we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that lets parents record their voice so children can hear bedtime stories narrated by someone they love. We take your privacy seriously and have written this policy in plain language so you always know exactly how your information is handled.

1. Who We Are

storynest is developed and operated by Kennedy Anderson, an independent developer. We can be reached at storynest.hello@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to make the app work:

  • Voice recordings. When you record your voice in-app, that recording is transmitted to ElevenLabs (our AI voice processing partner) to generate a cloned voice model. We do not retain the original recording on our own servers.
  • App settings and preferences. Things like playback speed, sleep timer settings, accent color, and child profiles are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). We have no access to this data.
  • Subscription status. We receive a signal from Apple's StoreKit confirming whether your subscription is active. We never see your payment details, Apple ID, or billing information.
  • Notification permission. If you enable bedtime reminders, iOS stores your permission locally. We do not collect or transmit this information.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, device identifiers, or browsing history. There are no user accounts and no sign-in.

3. Voice Data & ElevenLabs

Your voice recording is sent to ElevenLabs solely to create a voice model. storynest does not store your voice on its own servers. Once ElevenLabs has created the voice model, the original audio sample is deleted by ElevenLabs in accordance with their data retention policies.

Here is the full flow:

  1. You record your voice inside storynest.
  2. The recording is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to ElevenLabs' API.
  3. ElevenLabs uses the recording to train a voice model uniquely associated with your device.
  4. storynest calls ElevenLabs to generate story narrations using that voice model.
  5. Generated audio files are saved only on your device.

ElevenLabs is a third-party service with its own privacy practices. We strongly encourage you to review their policy at elevenlabs.io/privacy before recording your voice.

To delete your voice model from ElevenLabs, you may contact them directly through their support channels or through the storynest app (which sends a deletion request on your behalf).

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

storynest is designed to be operated by parents and caregivers, not by children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  • Parents record their own voice and control the app.
  • Children are listeners — they are not asked to enter information, create profiles, or interact with data-collection features.
  • Child profile names and emoji (created by parents) are stored locally on the parent's device only and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at storynest.hello@gmail.com and we will take prompt corrective action.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above only to:

  • Generate voice-cloned story narrations (via ElevenLabs).
  • Save your preferences and stories locally on your device.
  • Confirm your subscription status to unlock premium features.
  • Send bedtime reminder notifications (only if you enable this).

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

6. Data Storage

All generated narrations and preferences are stored locally on your device. storynest does not operate cloud servers or databases. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed. To also remove your voice model from ElevenLabs, follow the voice deletion steps in the app or contact ElevenLabs directly.

7. Third Parties

storynest works with the following third parties:

  • ElevenLabs — voice processing and text-to-speech generation. See their privacy policy.
  • Apple — in-app subscription management and payment processing. See Apple's privacy policy.

We do not share your data with any other third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

8. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Premium features are available via a monthly subscription processed entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment method, credit card number, or Apple ID. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status from Apple's StoreKit.

9. Notifications

If you enable bedtime reminders, the app uses iOS local notifications, which run entirely on your device. We do not use push notification services or remote servers to deliver reminders.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information handled in connection with the app:

  • All communication with ElevenLabs uses HTTPS (TLS encryption).
  • storynest's App Transport Security settings are locked to only permit connections to api.elevenlabs.io, blocking all other unencrypted traffic.
  • Locally stored data is protected by iOS device encryption.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data. To the extent any data is collected, you may:

  • Request deletion of your ElevenLabs voice model.
  • Delete all locally stored app data by uninstalling the app.
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns.

For requests, email us at storynest.hello@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to check this page occasionally.
